About the Event

Celebrating Every Woman

✿

Astitva — meaning "existence" — is a celebration of womanhood in all its beauty, power, and grace. This event honors the essence of every woman who crafts, creates, and conquers.

Organized by the Women's Cell of Global College of BBA & BCA, this two-day cultural extravaganza brings together talented women for a series of artistic, culinary, and performing arts competitions.

Join us on March 3rd & 4th, 2026 as we celebrate Strength, Style, and Spirit!
